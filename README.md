## 🎯 Project Overview
A comprehensive sales analytics dashboard built 100% in Microsoft Excel to track and analyze e-commerce sales performance across multiple dimensions. This interactive dashboard leverages Excel's powerful features to provide actionable insights into revenue patterns, product performance, and profitability across different geographical regions and product categories.

---

## 🧰 My Toolkit
Tool	What I Used It For
📗 Excel	Data cleaning, pivot tables, formulas (e.g., SUMIFS, XLOOKUP), charts, and interactive dashboard design

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components
This Excel dashboard features 5 key analyses:

Chart / KPI	Insight
💰 Key Metrics (KPIs)	At-a-glance view of Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Order Value (AOV), and Total Profit
📦 Quantity per Category	See which product categories (Electronics, Accessories, Office) sell the most units
🏆 Top 5 Products	Identify the best-selling products based on quantity sold
📈 Revenue Trend	Track sales and profit trends over time (by month/year)
🌍 Sales & Profit by Region	Compare performance across East, West, North, and South regions

## 📊 Key Insights (2022-2024)

### 📅 **3-Year Trend Analysis**

#### 2022: Foundation Year
- **Sales Volume:** Moderate but steady
- **Key Products:** Monitors and Mice lead the way
- **Regional Focus:** West region shows early promise
- **Learning:** Electronics category shows highest potential

#### 2023: Growth Acceleration
- **YoY Growth:** +22.7% in sales
- **Star Performer:** Smartwatch enters top 5 with 1,807 units
- **Category Expansion:** Accessories show 35% growth
- **Regional Development:** East region catches up to West

#### 2024: Peak Performance
- **Sales Peak:** Highest monthly sales recorded
- **Product Mix:** Balanced across all categories
- **Regional Maturity:** All regions show consistent performance
- **Market Position:** Strong foothold in Electronics and Accessories

### 🎯 **Product Performance Insights**

#### Top 5 Products Deep Dive

| Rank | Product | Units (2022-24) | Peak Year | Key Insight |
|------|---------|-----------------|-----------|-------------|
| 🥇 | **Monitor** | 1,876 | 2023 | Consistent #1, back-to-school drives Q3 sales |
| 🥈 | **Smartwatch** | 1,807 | 2023 | Fastest growing (launched late 2022), Q4 holiday peak |
| 🥉 | **Camera** | 1,795 | 2023 | Strong 2023, slight dip in 2024 due to smartphone cameras |
| 4th | **Mouse** | 1,753 | 2023 | Essential peripheral, gaming variants drive premium |
| 5th | **Printer** | 1,734 | 2023 | Office essential, work-from-home sustain demand |

#### Category Performance

**Electronics** 🎮 *(65% of total sales)*
- Monitor, Smartwatch, Camera in top 5
- High average order value ($850+)
- Tech enthusiasts and professionals core audience

**Accessories** 🎧 *(25% of total sales)*
- Mouse leading the category
- High margin, impulse purchases
- Cross-sell success with Electronics

**Office Supplies** 📎 *(10% of total sales)*
- Printer sole representative in top 5
- B2B accounts driving volume
- Recurring revenue from printer ink

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

### By Region

#### **West Region** 🏆
- Capitalize on Electronics dominance (54%)
- Revive Accessories sales (declined to 37%)
- Maintain stable Office supplies

#### **East Region** 📈
- Balance Electronics (48%) and Accessories (42%)
- Grow Office segment (10%)
- Stabilize profit around $155K

#### **South Region** 🌱
- Investigate 2024 profit drop (-21%)
- Leverage Accessories strength (40%)
- Maintain Office leadership (11%)

#### **North Region** 🚀

- Target $160K+ profit again

1. **Best Practice Sharing**
